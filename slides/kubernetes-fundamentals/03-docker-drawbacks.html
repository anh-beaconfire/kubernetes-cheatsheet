Why Kubernetes Exists

Docker Drawbacks

Docker Engine Docker Compose Docker Swarm
  • Single-host only: no clustering or multi-node support
  • No scheduler or workload placement
  • No cross-node self-healing
  • No service discovery or multi-host networking
  • No autoscaling or declarative desired state
  • Limited monitoring and lifecycle management
  • Cannot span multiple machines
  • No rolling updates or zero-downtime deployments
  • No self-healing or autoscaling
  • No cluster networking or service discovery
  • No RBAC, quotas, or multi-tenant isolation
  • Limited ecosystem and community adoption
  • Weak autoscaling with no HPA equivalent
  • Basic scheduling with limited affinity and anti-affinity
  • No CRDs, operators, or extensibility
  • Limited observability and policy enforcement
  • Not suitable for large-scale or enterprise workloads
