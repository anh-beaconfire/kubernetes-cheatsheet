Why Do We Need Kubernetes?

  • Modern apps are distributed and containerized
  • Services must scale independently
  • Infrastructure is dynamic and failures are inevitable
  • Deployments must be consistent and automated
  • Teams need a standard platform for running workloads
  • Production requires self‑healing and resilience
  • Autoscaling must be automatic and policy‑driven
  • Networking, service discovery, and load balancing must be built‑in
  • Configs and secrets must be managed securely
  • Observability must be unified across the system
  • Workloads must run reliably across clusters and environments
  • Organizations need portability across clouds and on‑prem
