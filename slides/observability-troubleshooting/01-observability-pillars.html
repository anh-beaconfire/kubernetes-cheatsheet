Observability Pillars

  • Observability helps you understand system behavior by exposing what your services are doing internally.
  • Logs — detailed event records for debugging and root‑cause analysis.
  • Metrics — numeric time‑series data for performance, SLOs, and alerting.
  • Traces — end‑to‑end request flows across microservices.
  • Events — Kubernetes lifecycle signals (Pod restarts, scheduling issues).
  • Health checks — readiness/liveness signals for service reliability.
  • Prometheus — metrics collection and alerting engine.
  • Grafana — visualization and dashboards for metrics and traces.
