Scaling

Scheduling: NodeSelector, Affinity, Taints, Tolerations

  • nodeSelector: simple node matching
  • affinity: advanced scheduling preference/requirement
  • taints: repel pods
  • tolerations: allow pods onto tainted nodes
  • use labels consistently
  • avoid over-constraining scheduling
nodeSelector:
  workload: gpu

---

tolerations:
  - key: "dedicated"
    operator: "Equal"
    value: "platform"
    effect: "NoSchedule"
