Workloads

Job

  • A Job runs Pods until a task completes successfully, then stops
  • Ensures completion: retries failed Pods based on backoff policy
  • Designed for finite, one‑time, or batch workloads
  • Common use cases: batch processing, database migrations, one‑time scripts, data import/export
  • Jobs should be idempotent — they may run more than once
  • Use restartPolicy: Never or OnFailure for correct behavior
  • For recurring tasks, use a CronJob instead
apiVersion: batch/v1
kind: Job
metadata:
  name: db-migration
spec:
  backoffLimit: 3
  template:
    spec:
      restartPolicy: Never
      containers:
        - name: migrate
          image: myorg/migration:1.0
          command: ["npm", "run", "migrate"]
