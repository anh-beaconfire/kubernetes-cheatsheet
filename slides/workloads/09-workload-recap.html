Workload Recap

Situation Choose
Stateless app, API, or web service Deployment
Requires stable identity, ordered startup, or persistent storage StatefulSet
One Pod per node (or per selected nodes) DaemonSet
Run to completion once (finite task) Job
Run on a recurring schedule CronJob
Lowest-level unit; used inside controllers Pod
  • Controllers manage desired state — Deployments, StatefulSets, DaemonSets, Jobs, and CronJobs create, replace, and reconcile Pods automatically.
  • Pods are ephemeral — they are disposable units that can restart, reschedule, or be replaced at any time.
