Pod

  • A Pod is the smallest deployable unit in Kubernetes
  • Wraps one or more tightly‑coupled containers
  • Containers share network namespace, IP, and storage volumes
  • Pods have a single lifecycle — containers start, run, and die together
  • Not ideal to create standalone Pods in production
  • Use controllers (Deployment, StatefulSet, Job, DaemonSet) to manage Pods
  • Controllers handle scaling, rollout, and self‑healing
  • Define resource requests/limits for scheduling and stability
  • Pods are ephemeral — they can be recreated at any time
apiVersion: v1
kind: Pod
metadata:
  name: nginx-pod
  labels:
    app: nginx
spec:
  containers:
    - name: nginx
      image: nginx:1.27
      ports:
        - containerPort: 80
